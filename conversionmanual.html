How to convert your powerbank into a charging station?

⚠️ Safety warning: Ensure your power bank is fully discharged (0%) before beginning. Do not use metal knives or sharp tools.
  1. The Unlock: Locate the standard micro-screws at the base of the device. Using the provided bit, remove the screws to release the snap-fit exterior shell.
  2. The Core Extraction: Gently open the casing. You will see the battery shell and the magnetic core module. Carefully disconnect the solderless plug (JST connector) linking the two halves.
  3. The Upgrade: Take the intact magnetic core module and press it into your weighted desktop dock. Plug the USB-C cable directly into the core.
  4. Power On: Your device is now a permanent, your own MagSafe charging station.
