About us

Globally, millions of perfectly functioning neodymium magnets and copper induction coils are thrown into landfills every year simply because the chemical battery attached to them degrades. We believe your tech should adapt, not expire. Our modular MagSafe Power Bank is engineered without industrial adhesives, adhering to strict Design for Disassembly principles. When the portable battery reaches its end-of-life, the high-value magnetic core is designed to be easily extracted and reborn as a premium, stationary desktop charger. You aren't just buying a power bank; you are investing in a permanent piece of tech infrastructure.
